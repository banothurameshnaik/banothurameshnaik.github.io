Request for Delete Account

You can request deleting your account permanently by emailing us at email rameshnaikprojects@gmail.com

Follow below steps for requesting account deletion.


  • Write an email to us at rameshnaikprojects@gmail.com
  • Email subject should be "Milch App - Request for Account Deletion"
  • You are required to provide your "Phone Number" which was used to create Milch Application account in the email body


Note:

  • Once we receive above request for deleting your account
  • Your account will be deleted within 48 hours
  • Your account deletion means "Permanent deletion"
  • You cannot access Milch application anymore
  • All the data belongs to your account (access details, records etc.) will be deleted permanently.

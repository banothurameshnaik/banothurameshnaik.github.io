Milch

Manage Milk Register Entries Online.

Overview

Milch is an application for milk agents (Milk collectors from farmers) to manage their daily activities of customers milk entries through online and go for paperless works.


Background

The actual milk collectors will buy milk from direct farmers / villages on daily basis. And they transport to nearest milk dairy(s). Milk collectors usally called as Milk Agents.

Payment goes to customers from Milk Agents usally in two methods

Method: 1 - Daily and on the go payment

In this method, milk agents will pay the cost of milk to customers instantaneously. Calculation of payments goes with manual process using calculators. This will be a time consuming process when number of customers are more a time and difficult to manage.

Method: 2 - Periodic payment

In this method, milk agents will collect the milk from customers on daily basis. But the payment will be done once a periodic like monthly/weekly.

So in this method milk agents are required to note down customer and milk details (quantity, quality) once they purchase milk from customer daily.

To maintain this history of details, milk agents usally go for books / paper based and on periodic basic they will do the calculation of amounts which needs to pay all the customers. and pay the actual amount to each customer.

Milch App is on the way to help here

We can see that either of the above methods milk agents follow, they are going to face some difficulties to maintain data and business.

Yes, to remove the repeated tasks, calculations and easy the works for milk agents, Milch App.

Using Milch App, milk agent works will be done in easy way and mainly paperless.

Who can use this App?

The actual milk collectors (Usally called as Milk Agents)

Features

  1. Made for milk agents
  2. Paperless & Easy to use.
  3. Instant Payment Calculation
  4. Manage Daily Records Online
  5. Download customer reports
  6. Save time & cost

How it use?

Milk agents can use Milch App easily.

  1. Register with Milch App by downloading it from Playstore
  2. Enter your customer details only once.
  3. On daily basic you can start using it by adding milk entries
  4. You can view/download reports at any time!
